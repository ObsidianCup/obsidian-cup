The rules

Tournament Rules

U19 Boys — Eligibility

◇Born on or after 1 January 2007. Mixed-school teams are permitted.
◇No players who have represented any Bangladesh national volleyball team at any level, including all youth and age-group teams.
◇No active members of defense services sports teams — Army, Navy, Ansar, or any paramilitary forces.
◇Valid photo ID with date of birth required. If ID lacks photo or DOB, also upload a NID, Birth Certificate, or Passport.

Boys Open Age — Eligibility

◇All ages. No age restrictions.
◇No players currently or previously selected for the Bangladesh senior national volleyball team.
◇No active members of defense services sports teams — Army, Navy, Ansar, or any paramilitary forces.
◇Valid photo ID required: School/College ID, NID, or Passport.

Girls Open Age — Eligibility

◇All ages. Amateur only.
◇No players who have represented Bangladesh national women's volleyball team at any level — including all youth and age-group teams.
◇No players registered with or currently representing any national women's volleyball league club.
◇No active members of defense services sports teams — Army, Navy, Ansar, or any paramilitary forces.
◇Valid photo ID required: School/College ID, NID, or Passport.

Squad Requirements

◇Registered roster: minimum 6, maximum 12 players.
◇Up to 2 liberos per team. Libero jersey must clearly contrast all other players at all times.
◇No two players on the same team may share a jersey number.
◇All players must wear matching jerseys on match day. Non-matching or numberless attire = barred from court.
◇Captain and vice-captain must be designated before the tournament begins. A libero may serve as captain.

Match Format

◇Group Stage: round-robin. Best of 3 sets — Sets 1 & 2 to 25 pts, Set 3 to 15 pts. 2-point advantage required, no point cap.
◇Match points: Win 2–0 = 3 pts  /  Win 2–1 = 2 pts + 1 pt  /  Forfeit = 3 pts + 0 pts.
◇Tiebreaker order: head-to-head result → sets ratio → points ratio → drawing of lots.
◇Knockout Stage: top teams advance. Single-elimination semi-finals and final.
◇2 timeouts per set (30 sec each). Maximum 6 substitutions per set — libero substitutions not counted.

Match Day & Conduct

◇Teams must be at the assigned court with minimum 6 registered players at least 15 minutes before scheduled match time.
◇Failure to field within 15 minutes of scheduled start = forfeit awarded to the opposing team.
◇Match officials' decisions are final and binding. Dissent toward officials will not be tolerated.
◇Abusive language, physical altercation, smoking, alcohol consumption, or vandalism: immediate removal from venue.
◇Disqualification for conduct violations = no refund of registration fee.
