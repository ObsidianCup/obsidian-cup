Tournament Rules

You must read and acknowledge the rules before registering

1. Eligibility

All participating players must meet the eligibility criteria specified for the tournament category (interschool age restrictions for OVC, open age for OVASC). Team managers or captains are responsible for ensuring all players are eligible.

2. Team Composition

Teams must register a minimum of 10 and a maximum of 12 players. A valid team roster must be submitted during registration with identification documents for each player.

3. Identification

Each player must provide a valid scanned copy of one of the following: National ID Card, Passport, or current institution Student ID. Failure to provide valid identification will result in disqualification of the player or team.

4. Match Rules

All matches will follow standard FIVB volleyball rules unless otherwise specified. The tournament committee reserves the right to modify rules for specific rounds and will communicate any changes in advance.

5. Conduct

All players, coaches, and team representatives must conduct themselves with respect and sportsmanship at all times. Any form of unsportsmanlike conduct, verbal abuse, or physical altercation will result in immediate disqualification.

6. Punctuality

Teams must be present at the venue at least 30 minutes before their scheduled match. Failure to appear within 15 minutes of the scheduled match time will result in a forfeit.

7. Registration & Payment

Registration is only confirmed upon successful payment via bKash and verification by the tournament committee. Slots are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Payment not confirmed within 48 hours of submission will result in the slot being released.

8. Photography & Media

By participating in Obsidian Cup, players and teams consent to being photographed and filmed during the tournament. Media may be used for promotional purposes on official Obsidian Cup channels.

9. Committee Decision

The tournament committee's decisions on all matters are final. Any disputes must be raised formally through the official team representative and will be addressed by the committee.

10. Changes & Updates

Obsidian Cup reserves the right to make changes to the schedule, venue, or rules. All registered teams will be notified via their registered contact information and through the official Instagram @obsidiancup.
