Eligibility

01Open to all ages. Girls only.
02Strictly amateur. Players who have represented Bangladesh at national, divisional, army, or fire service level are not eligible.
03Valid photo ID required. Accepted: School or University ID with photo, NID, or Passport.
04We are an independent community tournament and rely on honesty and community reporting. Where valid proof of ineligibility is provided, the player or team may be removed. Where no verifiable evidence exists, results stand.

Squad

05Minimum 10, maximum 12 players per roster. Matches are 6-a-side including goalkeeper.
06Unlimited rolling substitutions through the designated zone.
07Matching jerseys required. Goalkeeper must wear a clearly different colour. Jersey numbers must be unique per team.
08Captain and vice-captain must be designated before the tournament begins.

Match Rules

09Standard AFC/FIFA futsal rules apply. Kick-ins replace throw-ins. Restarts within 4 seconds.
10Two yellow cards or a direct red = player sent off. Team plays short-handed for 2 minutes or until opponent scores.
11Minimum 6 players required at kickoff. Failure to field may result in walkover at organiser's discretion. Referee decisions are final.

Conduct

12Violence, abusive behaviour, cheating, or serious misconduct may result in match bans or removal from the tournament.
13Obsidian Cup will be photographed and filmed throughout. By registering, all players consent to their images being used for documentation and promotion.
