About Obsidian Cup

What is Obsidian Cup?

Obsidian Cup is a volleyball tournament brand based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We run OVC (Obsidian Volleyball Cup) and OVASC (Obsidian Volleyball All Stars Cup) — two distinct tournament formats designed to give players at every level a proper, well-run competitive experience. We care deeply about quality — the venues, the production, the atmosphere — and we're building something that lasts.

Who runs Obsidian Cup?

Obsidian Cup is founded and run by Uriana, with Tafhim on the field and a core founding circle of Masheer, Nabid, and Rifa. No corporate backing, no large team — just people who love the game and are committed to doing it right.

How many seasons have you run?

Three seasons across two tournament formats. OVC Season 1 in January 2026, OVASC Season 1 in February 2026, and the Obsidian Volleyball Super Cup in April 2026 — which brought both formats together on one stage for the first time. Over 26 teams and 290 players have competed so far.

What sports do you cover?

Volleyball is where we started. But our vision extends beyond it — we want to build a home for underground sport in Bangladesh across the board. More sports, more athletes, more stages are part of the plan.

Registration and Payment

Does filling the registration form confirm my team's spot?

No. Submitting the registration form is only the first step. Your slot is never confirmed until payment is received and we have issued you an invoice. After submitting the form, you must send the registration fee via bKash (Send Money) using your team name as reference, then email your transaction ID and screenshot to obsidiancup.official@gmail.com. Only then is your slot confirmed.

What happens if I pay but don't send the transaction details?

Your slot will not be confirmed. We cannot match a payment to a team without the transaction ID and screenshot. Always email us immediately after paying.

What is the refund policy?

If your team cannot participate, send a written cancellation via email at least 24 hours before the event and we will issue a 50% refund. No refund is issued after this window.

Can we change a player after registering?

Before the registration deadline, yes — contact us via Instagram, Facebook, or email. After the deadline, changes are at our discretion and decided on a case by case basis.

Events and Match Day

Is there an entry fee for spectators?

No. All Obsidian Cup events are free for spectators. Come and support.

Will photos and videos be taken at events?

Yes. All Obsidian Cup events are photographed and filmed. By participating or attending, players and teams consent to their images and videos being used for documentation and promotion across our social media channels.

What happens if an event is cancelled or the venue changes?

In such cases we will notify all registered teams immediately via email and social media. If the event cannot be rescheduled, a full refund will be issued.
