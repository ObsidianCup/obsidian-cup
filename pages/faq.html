FAQ

Everything you need to know before you show up.

Registration and Payment

Does submitting the form confirm my team's spot?

No. Submitting the form is only the first step. Your slot is confirmed once we receive your payment and email you an invoice. Send 3,800 BDT via bKash (Send Money) to 01791341727, use your team name as reference, then email your transaction ID and screenshot to obsidiancup.official@gmail.com. We will confirm within 3 days.

What is the registration deadline?

The deadline is 27th March 2026. Registrations received after this date are subject to a late fee of 4,000 BDT per team, and are subject to slot availability.

Can we change a player after registering?

If the registration deadline has not passed, yes. Contact us via Instagram, Facebook, or email and we will make the change. After the deadline, player changes are at our discretion and will be decided on a case by case basis.

What is the refund policy?

If your team cannot participate, send us a written cancellation via email at least 24 hours before the event and we will issue a 50% refund. No refund is issued after this window.

Categories and Eligibility

Can a Boys Interschool player also play in Boys Open Age?

Yes. An interschool player may be registered on a Boys Open Age team as well. However, we cannot guarantee that match timings will not overlap. If matches are scheduled at the same time, the player must choose which match to play. This is at the player's own risk.

Can a Boys Open Age player join an Interschool team?

No. Open Age players who are not currently enrolled in school are not eligible for the Boys Interschool category.

What counts as valid ID for players?

For Boys Interschool: current session school ID with a photo. If the school ID has no photo, also upload a supporting photo ID matching the same details (Passport or Exam Admit Card). For Boys Open Age and Girls Open Age: NID, Passport, or any institutional ID card with a photo (student card, work ID, or similar). A photo is mandatory on all submitted IDs for face verification.

What does amateur mean for the Girls Open Age category?

Any player who has represented Bangladesh at divisional, national, or any institutional level (including but not limited to army, ansar, or similar organisations) in any official volleyball competition is not eligible. This category is for players who play for the love of the game, not at an official competitive level.

Match Day

What time does the tournament start?

Matches begin at 8:00 AM on both days. A detailed fixture schedule will be released once registration closes. Follow @obsidiancup on Instagram and Facebook to stay updated.

What format will the tournament follow?

The format will be announced after registration closes, once we know the total number of teams in each category. Follow our social media for the update.

What happens if a team shows up with fewer than 6 players?

A minimum of 6 players must be present to start a match per FIVB regulations. If a team cannot field 6 players, the match outcome will be decided at the discretion of the organiser. In some cases, if the opposing team agrees, a reduced format may be allowed.

Is there an entry fee for spectators?

No. Spectators are welcome to attend free of charge.

Will there be food or water available?

Water will be provided for all registered players. There will be no food or outside refreshment arrangements on the premises. Players are encouraged to bring their own snacks.

What should teams wear?

All players must wear matching jerseys. The libero must wear a jersey of a clearly contrasting colour from the rest of the team. Jersey numbers must be between 1 and 20 with no two players sharing a number.

Other Questions

What happens if the venue changes or the event is cancelled?

In the unlikely event of a venue change or cancellation due to circumstances beyond our control, we will notify all registered teams immediately via email and our social media channels. If the event cannot be rescheduled, a full refund will be issued.

Will photos and videos be taken at the event?

Yes. Obsidian Cup will be photographed and filmed throughout both days. By registering, all players and team members consent to their images and videos being used for tournament documentation and promotional purposes across our social media channels.
