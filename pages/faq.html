About Obsidian Cup

What is Obsidian Cup?

Obsidian Cup is a multi-sport tournament brand based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We started with volleyball and have now expanded to football. We run events designed to give players at every level a proper, well-organised competitive experience — real venues, real production, real care.

Who runs Obsidian Cup?

Founded and run by Uriana, with Tafhim on the field and a founding circle of Masheer, Nabid, and Rifa. No corporate backing, no large team — just people who love sport and show up every time.

What sports do you run events for?

Volleyball and football so far. Volleyball has three completed seasons across OVC, OVASC, and the Super Cup. Football launches with OFC Season 1 in June 2026. More sports are part of the long-term plan.

Where can I follow Obsidian Cup?

Football updates: @obsidian.football on Instagram. Volleyball updates: @obsidian.volleyball on Instagram. All sports and general Obsidian news on our Facebook page.

Registration and Payment

Does filling the registration form confirm my team's spot?

No. Your slot is never confirmed until payment is received and we issue an invoice. After submitting the form, send the registration fee via bKash (Send Money) to 01711878945 using your team name as reference, then email your transaction ID and screenshot to obsidiancup.official@gmail.com. Only then is your slot confirmed.

What happens if I pay but don't send the transaction details?

Your slot will not be confirmed. We cannot match a payment to a team without the transaction ID and screenshot. Always email us immediately after paying.

What is the refund policy?

Send a written cancellation via email to obsidiancup.official@gmail.com at least 24 hours before the event for a 50% refund. No refund is issued after this window.

Can we change a player after registering?

Before the registration deadline, yes — contact us via Instagram or email. After the deadline, changes are at our discretion and decided case by case.

Events and Match Day

Is there an entry fee for spectators?

No. All Obsidian Cup events are free for spectators. Come and support.

Will photos and videos be taken at events?

Yes. All Obsidian Cup events are photographed and filmed. By registering, players and teams consent to their images and videos being used for documentation and promotion across our social media channels.

What happens if an event is cancelled or the venue changes?

We will notify all registered teams immediately via email and social media. If the event cannot be rescheduled, a full refund will be issued.
